Popular Destinations

Explore Mumbai

Explore Mumbai's vibrant mix of cultures, from historic landmarks like the Gateway of India to bustling markets like Crawford Market. Stroll along Marine Drive for stunning views of the Arabian Sea or delve into the city's culinary scene, offering everything from street food to fine dining.

Amazing Maldives

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Maldives, with its pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life. Dive into world-class snorkeling and diving spots, relax in luxurious overwater bungalows, and indulge in exquisite seafood cuisine amidst stunning island scenery.

Beautiful Kalimpong

Kalimpong, nestled in the Himalayan foothills, offers breathtaking mountain vistas, lush greenery, and a tranquil ambiance. Explore its vibrant bazaars, Buddhist monasteries, and colonial-era architecture. Enjoy adventurous trekking trails, serene tea gardens, and warm hospitality, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts alike.
